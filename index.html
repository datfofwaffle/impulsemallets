Built from the inside out

Impulse Mallets started with a simple question: why does quality gear have to cost so much? As a competitive tenor drummer, I know what a well-balanced mallet feels like — and I know most players never get access to it.

Every set is designed with real playing experience behind it. Precision-printed shafts, hand-wrapped heads, and honest materials. No shortcuts, no markup for a name.
